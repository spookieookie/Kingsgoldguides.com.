# Herbalism Farming: Best Routes and Spots

## Introduction

Herbalism is one of the most consistent gold-making professions in World of Warcraft. With the right routes and timing, you can earn 2,500-3,200 gold per hour farming high-demand herbs in Khaz Algar.

## Best Herbalism Routes

### Primary Route: Nerub-ar Circuit
The most efficient route starts at the Nerub-ar Palace entrance and circles through the Spiral Veil. This loop:
- Takes 6-8 minutes per complete rotation
- Yields 30-40 Hochenblume per lap
- Requires flying or significant ground travel time
- Best done during off-peak server hours

### Secondary Route: Ringing Deeps Farming
If the primary route is crowded:
- Head to the Ringing Deeps zone
- Focus on the western quadrant near Deepkom
- Slightly lower density than Nerub-ar
- Often less contested

## Tips for Maximum Efficiency

1. **Clear Your Quest Log**: Drop unwanted quests to maximize screen space and minimize clutter
2. **Use Gathering Addons**: WeakAuras or similar addons can help track herb spawn points
3. **Farm During Off-Peak Hours**: 3 AM - 9 AM server time typically has the least competition
4. **Route Optimization**: Minimize backtracking by following a logical path
5. **Mailbox Management**: Use a second character near farming zones to mail herbs

## Materials Market

Current market prices vary by server, but Hochenblume typically sells for:
- **Individual Herb**: 8-15 gold each
- **Stacks of 20**: 160-300 gold per stack
- **As Potions**: 30-45 gold per unit (with alchemist cut)
