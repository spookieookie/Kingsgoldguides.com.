# 10,000 Gold in 10 Minutes - Timeless Isle Condensed Jademist Farm

## Quick Summary

This World of Warcraft guide covers a quick Timeless Isle farm at **The Misty Strand**. The route focuses on killing misty elemental mobs near the shoreline and geysers for **Timeless Coins, Lesser Charms, and the valuable Condensed Jademist drop**.

The original King Kunta video frames the farm as a **10,000 gold in 10 minutes opportunity**. The website should make the value proposition clear: **this is a fast, easy route with market-dependent upside, not guaranteed raw gold**.

## Why This Farm Works

The Misty Strand on Timeless Isle features:
- **High mob density** with quick respawns
- **Simple route** on a tight shoreline loop
- **Valuable drop** in Condensed Jademist (if your realm has demand)
- **Bonus loot** in Timeless Coins and Lesser Charms

The key angle is that **Condensed Jademist can create the gold value** behind the short-session claim. This is not guaranteed vendor gold—it depends on auction-house demand and pricing.

## Step-by-Step Route

### Step 1: Travel to Timeless Isle
Go to Pandaria and reach Timeless Isle. Use the flight paths from Vale of Eternal Blossoms if needed.

### Step 2: Position at The Misty Strand
Move to the shoreline area with geysers, rocks, and misty elemental mobs. This is the western shore of Timeless Isle, identifiable by the geyser vents and spirit/elemental packs.

### Step 3: Kill the Elemental Mobs Around the Geysers
Pull and kill the nearby mist-style mobs as they appear. Keep moving between the clustered spawns around the shoreline, rocks, and small spirit/elemental packs. Combat is straightforward—most players can handle these mobs without issue.

### Step 4: Loot Every Mob
The visible loot includes:
- **Timeless Coins** (vendor value + collectible demand)
- **Lesser Charm of Good Fortune** (collectible)
- **Condensed Jademist** (auction-house value)

Loot everything because the value comes from item drops, not just direct coin rewards.

### Step 5: Reset Your Route and Repeat
Once the immediate pack is cleared, circle through the same shoreline area and pick up respawns. Keep the loop tight instead of roaming the entire island. One full 10-minute loop should yield substantial drops if Condensed Jademist has a buyer on your realm.

## Loot & Gold Logic

**Condensed Jademist is the standout visible drop** in the reviewed video. Timeless Coins and Lesser Charms add side value, but the larger gold result depends on collectible/item demand.

**Reality check**: The 10,000 gold in 10 minutes claim depends on the drop value and whether the item sells. This is **not guaranteed vendor gold**. Frame the result as a **short-session, market-dependent farm**.

Because this is a short-session farm, **test the route for 10 minutes, track drops, then decide whether to keep farming**. If the valuable drop is not moving on the auction house, the farm may not be worth extended time.

## Auction House Strategy

- **Check Condensed Jademist pricing before you farm** to gauge the market.
- **Do not undercut aggressively** if the item has low supply. Rare/collectible drops can take time to sell.
- **List collectible drops with patience, not panic pricing.**
- Use Timeless Coins and side loot as **bonus value, not the main value claim**.
- **Mention that sale speed varies heavily by realm and market timing.**

## Reality Expectations

This farm works best when:
1. You have 10-15 minutes for a quick session
2. Condensed Jademist has active buyers on your realm
3. You are comfortable with auction-house selling delays

This farm does **not** work well if:
- Condensed Jademist is price-crashed on your realm
- You need immediate raw gold (this is not a direct vendor farm)
- You prefer guaranteed gold over material flipping
