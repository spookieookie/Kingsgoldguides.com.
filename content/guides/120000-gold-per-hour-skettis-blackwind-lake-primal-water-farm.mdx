# 120,000 Gold Per Hour - Skettis Blackwind Lake Primal Water Farm

## Quick Summary

This World of Warcraft gold farm targets **Skettis Surgers at Blackwind Lake in Terokkar Forest**. The route is simple: **swim through the lake, kill water elementals, loot Mote of Water, and use the auction house to turn the drops into gold**.

The original King Kunta video positions this as a **120,000 gold per hour instant-spawn farm**. The best way to present it is as a **high-upside, market-dependent elemental material farm, not as a guaranteed raw-gold method**.

## Why This Farm Works

Blackwind Lake in Skettis features:
- **Instant respawns** with no respawn timer between kills
- **High mob density** concentrated in a small water area
- **Consistent drops** in Mote of Water and Elemental Fragments
- **Valuable material** that spikes when players need crafted transmog or legacy recipes

The real result depends on **material prices, respawn density, competition, and sale-through rate**. This is **not a guaranteed raw-gold farm**—it is a **fast-repeat material farm**.

## Step-by-Step Route

### Step 1: Travel to Terokkar Forest
Go to Outland, then fly to Terokkar Forest. Head toward Shattrath City as your reference point. The video opens the map and shows the route into the Skettis area **southeast of Shattrath City**.

### Step 2: Move to Blackwind Lake
Head into Skettis and position yourself around **Blackwind Lake**. This is the green-water lake surrounded by arakkoa structures and trees. The farm is primarily **underwater and along the lake floor**.

### Step 3: Target Skettis Surgers
Kill **Skettis Surgers** as they appear. These water elementals are the target mobs for the farm. The video shows these around the lake, with loot including **Mote of Water and Elemental Fragment**.

**Pro tip**: Pull continuously and avoid standing around waiting for one spawn if another is nearby. The faster you cycle through kills, the more total motes you accumulate.

### Step 4: Loop the Underwater Spawn Pockets
Move between visible spawn clusters around the lake. The best route is a **loose underwater loop**:
1. Kill one pocket
2. Swim to the next
3. Loot everything
4. Rotate back as respawns appear

**Water-breathing utility** (Rogue abilities, Druid aquatic form, water-breathing potions) makes this much smoother than surface swimming.

### Step 5: Convert the Drops into Auction Value
The main economic point is the **Mote of Water / Primal Water value**. Check whether your realm rewards:
- Selling motes directly, or
- Combining 10 Motes into 1 Primal Water

Use the more profitable format for your market.

## Loot & Gold Logic

The visible loot in the video includes **Mote of Water and Elemental Fragment** from Skettis Surgers. The valuable component is **the elemental material**, because older expansion materials can **spike when players need crafted transmog, professions, or legacy recipes**.

**The farm works when three conditions line up**:
1. **Low competition** in the lake
2. **Fast respawns** (which Blackwind Lake has)
3. **Strong auction-house demand** for water materials

If any of those fail, the headline gold rate drops significantly.

**This farm is operationally simple but financially market-sensitive.** The best version of this guide should teach players to **test a 10-15 minute sample before committing a full hour**.

## Auction House Strategy

- **Check current Mote of Water and Primal Water pricing** before farming to gauge your expected income.
- **Calculate** whether 10 Mote of Water converted into 1 Primal Water is worth more than selling the motes individually.
- **Avoid flooding a thin market.** Post in controlled stacks that buyers actually use.
- **Repost during higher activity windows** when crafters and collectors are online.
- **Treat the 120k claim as upside potential, not a guaranteed hourly wage.**

## Reality Expectations

This farm works best when:
1. You can dedicate a full 60-minute session
2. Water elementals are in high demand on your realm
3. You have access to water-breathing or swim-speed utilities
4. You are comfortable with auction-house selling and material conversion

This farm does **not** work well if:
- Mote of Water prices are crashed on your realm
- You need immediate raw gold (this is a material farm, not a vendor farm)
- Underwater farming feels clunky without proper utilities
- Competition for spawns is heavy
