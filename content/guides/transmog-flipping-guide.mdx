# Transmog Flipping: Auction House Secrets

## What is Transmog Flipping?

Transmog flipping is buying cosmetic items (transmog) at below market value and reselling them to collectors and transmog enthusiasts for profit. It's one of the most lucrative gold-making methods if done correctly.

## The Strategy

### Identifying Profitable Items

Look for:
- Rare appearances from legacy content
- Items with low current auctions (0-3 available)
- Pieces with consistent buyer interest
- Gear from discontinued raids or dungeons

### Where to Find Deals

1. **Mass Transmog Listings**: Players often post large quantities of unsorted mog at low prices
2. **Bulk Lots**: Sellers liquidating inventories list items for quick sales
3. **Specific Hunts**: Target items you know have consistent demand

## Pricing Strategy

### The 3-Tier System

| Item Rarity | Buy Price | Sell Price | Target Profit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Common | 50-200g | 300-800g | 300-500% |
| Uncommon | 300-1000g | 2,000-5,000g | 200-400% |
| Rare | 1,000-5,000g | 15,000-50,000g | 150-200% |

## Tools of the Trade

- **TradeSkillMaster (TSM)**: The professional-grade tool for flipping
- **Auctionator**: Simpler but effective alternative
- **Price Check Add-ons**: Check current prices before buying

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Buying without research**: Always check current auction prices first
2. **Overstocking**: Don't hold more than 20% of the server's available stock
3. **Ignoring deposit fees**: Factor in AH fees when calculating profit
4. **Seasonal blindness**: Some items are seasonal; know your timing

## Advanced Techniques

- **Niche Collecting**: Target specific class appearances
- **Expansion Focuses**: Focus on one expansion's mog for deep expertise
- **Holiday Seasons**: Certain items spike during roleplay holidays
- **Raid Tier Targeting**: Focus on specific raid tiers that are "in" with collectors

## Getting Started

1. Start with 100,000 gold budget
2. Buy 10 underpriced items you're confident about
3. List them 5-10% below market average
4. Once they sell, reinvest profits and expand

The key to transmog flipping success is patience, research, and understanding what collectors actually want.
