## Auction House Selling Strategy

**Do not vendor green items automatically.** This is where the real value comes from.

1. **Separate vendor trash from AH candidates** - Check item tooltips for transmog potential
2. **Post valuable transmog items** at market-aware prices and avoid panic-undercutting
3. **Use 24-48 hour listings** depending on your realm reposting strategy
4. **Price check before posting** - Use TSM, Auctioneer, or realm-specific pricing data
5. **Long-tail inventory approach** - Some items may take days to sell, but that's normal for rare drops
6. **Don't judge by one run** - Rare-drop farms need repeated attempts and patience to see average value

### Reality Check

**Most individual kills are low-value.** The farm becomes interesting because locked chests and old-world BoE items can occasionally produce valuable auction-house inventory. This is not a "guaranteed gold generator"—it's a **strategy for patient farmers** who understand transmog markets.
