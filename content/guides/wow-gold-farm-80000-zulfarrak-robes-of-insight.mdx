## Auction House Strategy

This is a rare transmog and old-world BoE farm. The 80,000 gold headline comes from **potential Auction House value**, especially when Robes of Insight or another high-value item drops. **It is not guaranteed liquid gold.**

The correct strategy is to build a rare-item inventory and repost consistently:

- **Use an Auction House addon** (TradeSkillMaster, Auctionator) before vendoring any BoE drop.
- **Compare realm and regional value** before pricing Robes of Insight or other rares.
- **List as transmog/collector pieces**, not as leveling gear.
- **Avoid aggressive undercutting**. These items often have low volume but strong upside when the right buyer appears.
- **Use 24-48 hour listings** depending on deposit cost and reposting discipline.
- **Build a portfolio** of old-world transmog so one slow sale does not break the strategy.
- **Track actual sales**. Tooltip value and market value are not the same as gold in your mailbox.

The phrase "80,000 gold in one hour" is the video title claim and SEO angle, but **this site emphasizes the caveat**: A player may run Zul'Farrak for an hour and get mostly cloth and greens. Another player may hit Robes of Insight or a valuable BoE.

The business model is **rare-drop probability plus long-tail Auction House selling**. Do not expect 80k guaranteed raw gold every run. Treat it as upside potential when rare drops align with demand.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Calling the farm guaranteed 80k raw gold. It is not.
- Vendoring Robes of Insight or old-world green items without checking market value first.
- Running too slowly by killing mobs one at a time instead of grouping pulls.
- Ignoring the instance reset cap and wasting time after lockout.
- Undercutting rare transmog too hard just to force a fast sale.
- Only farming one item instead of building a broader transmog inventory.
