# Mining Ore Routes: Most Profitable Locations

## Overview

Mining remains the most stable gold-making profession for gatherers. With consistent ore prices and high demand from crafters, you can generate reliable income farming ore in Khaz Algar.

## Top Mining Locations

### Ringing Deeps - Primary Route
The Ringing Deeps hosts the highest concentration of Hochenium deposits:
- Dense cluster near the center of the zone
- 8-12 ore nodes visible at any given time
- Low player competition outside peak hours
- Good XP if you're still leveling

### Nerub-ar Palace Outskirts
A secondary but lucrative route:
- Higher-level ore spawn around the palace perimeter
- Mix of Hochenium and Vibrant Shard nodes
- More challenging terrain
- Lower competition than Ringing Deeps

## Ore Values

| Ore Type | Price Per Unit | Stack Price | Hourly Rate |
|----------|---|---|---|
| Hochenium | 12-18g | 240-360g | 3,500g |

## Pro Tips

- **Combine with other professions**: Herbs and ore often spawn near each other
- **Use two clients**: Farm on one account, flip on the other
- **Check your server**: Peak hours mean more competition but also better prices
- **Join a mining community**: Share route information for group farming
