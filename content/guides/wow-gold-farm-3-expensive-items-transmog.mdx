## Why This Farm Works

Rare transmog farms work because the supply side is thin. Most players do not repeatedly farm old dungeons and raids for obscure bind-on-equip weapons, so valuable appearances can be scarce on the Auction House. When a collector wants the exact appearance, the item can command a premium.

**This is not a farm where every run pays out.** Most of the time, you are creating chances at rare inventory. The correct business model is:
- **Volume**: Run the content repeatedly to build larger inventory
- **Patience**: Wait for the right buyer rather than panic-undercutting
- **Premium Pricing**: List valuable items at realistic premium prices, not vendor value
- **Consistency**: Repost items regularly and treat slow sale speed as part of the transmog business model

## Item 1: Advanced Flatbow

The Advanced Flatbow is a bind-on-equip bow with an old transmog appearance. The value comes from rarity and scarcity—players buy this kind of item because they want the exact look or because the item is hard to find listed.

### Location and Route
Travel to **Northrend** and enter **Gundrak** in Zul'Drak. Clear the trash route efficiently. The key is not boss mechanics—the key is mob volume: clear quickly, loot everything, and reset where the content rules allow it.

### Execution Steps
1. Go to Gundrak in Zul'Drak, Northrend
2. Enter the instance on a high-level character with fast AoE
3. Move through the main trash route and kill packs quickly
4. Loot every mob so rare BoE drops are not missed
5. Exit and reset where possible while respecting instance limits
6. Check all green and blue weapons before vendoring anything

### Expected Drops
- **Advanced Flatbow** (rare transmog bow)
- Uncommon and rare weapons
- Vendor trash and crafting materials
- Occasional transmog armor pieces

## Item 2: Crested Scepter

The Crested Scepter is a low-level bind-on-equip mace. Low-level transmog pieces can be valuable because many players vendor them without checking the market, and old dungeon supply can be thin.

### Location and Route
Travel to **Ashenvale** in Kalimdor and enter **Blackfathom Deeps**. Clear the early dungeon trash and continue through the cave-style route. The goal is to kill a large number of eligible mobs, not to focus on bosses.

### Execution Steps
1. Go to Ashenvale and enter Blackfathom Deeps
2. Clear the entrance and early interior packs quickly
3. Keep moving through the cave route instead of stopping between pulls
4. Loot everything and preserve all uncommon-quality weapons for price checks
5. Reset where possible and repeat if you are targeting this item specifically
6. Don't vendor low-level greens without market checking—some have surprising value

### Expected Drops
- **Crested Scepter** (rare transmog mace)
- Low-level transmog weapons and armor
- Uncommon BoE pieces
- Vendor trash and alchemical materials

## Item 3: Blade of Wizardry

The Blade of Wizardry is an older epic one-handed sword with strong collector and transmog appeal. This item appears in Karazhan trash and is worth farming specifically because epic transmog pieces have higher floors on the Auction House.

### Location and Route
Travel to **Deadwind Pass** in the Eastern Kingdoms and enter **Karazhan**. Move through early interior trash areas, including large open rooms where multiple enemies can be pulled and killed quickly. Karazhan is raid content, so reset behavior is not the same as a normal dungeon—treat this as an eligible-trash clear for rare BoE drops.

### Execution Steps
1. Go to Deadwind Pass and enter Karazhan
2. Move through the entrance and early interior trash packs
3. Pull safely, kill quickly, and loot every mob
4. Watch for epic BoE weapons and other old raid transmog pieces
5. Respect raid lockout behavior and avoid assuming dungeon-style reset rules
6. Map out efficient trash routes to maximize loot per lockout

### Expected Drops
- **Blade of Wizardry** (rare epic transmog sword)
- Epic and rare transmog armor
- Uncommon BoE weapons
- Enchanting materials and vendor loot

## Auction House Strategy

The Auction House strategy is the difference between making gold with this farm and wasting time. These items should be treated as premium transmog inventory.

| Action | Reason |
|--------|--------|
| Check realm and regional price | Rare transmog prices vary heavily by realm and supply |
| Avoid panic undercutting | Heavy undercutting destroys the upside that makes the farm worth running |
| Use 24–48 hour listings | Rare items need exposure time and consistent reposting |
| Build a larger inventory | One rare item can sit. Hundreds of rare items create regular sales chances |
| Keep realistic expectations | High listed value does not mean instant liquidity |
| Use Auction House addons | TradeSkillMaster or Auctionator for accurate pricing |

## Recommended Player Setup

- **Character**: High-level character that can one-shot old content
- **Stat Priority**: Movement speed and instant AoE over single-target damage
- **Bag Space**: Clear bags before starting to maximize loot
- **Tools**: Use an Auction House addon like TradeSkillMaster or Auctionator for pricing
- **Inventory**: Keep rare weapons, armor, recipes, and unusual low-level greens until price-checked

## Who This Farm Is For

This farm is best for players who:
- Like old-content farming and dungeon runs
- Enjoy transmog sales and Auction House inventory building
- Have patience for slow sale velocity
- Want to build a passive income stream from rare drops

This is **not ideal** for players who need fast liquid gold today. The upside is high-value sales; the tradeoff is RNG and slow sale velocity.

## Reality Check

- **Rare drops are genuinely rare**: You may farm for hours and get zero target items
- **Transmog sales are slow**: Listing an item at 5,000 gold does not mean it sells in a day
- **Prices fluctuate**: Realm and regional markets change weekly; reprice accordingly
- **Not guaranteed income**: Treat this as long-tail portfolio building, not a consistent farm
- **Volume matters**: One expensive item can sit; hundreds of rare items create regular sales chances
