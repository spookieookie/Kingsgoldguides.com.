## Farm 1: Mizjah Ruins Instant Respawn Ogre Farm

**Location:** Mizjah Ruins, Northern Stranglethorn, Eastern Kingdoms

**Mobs:** Mosh'Ogg Brute and surrounding Mosh'Ogg ogres

**Loot Profile:** Silver, copper, low-level greens, and rare transmog/world-drop chances

### How the Farm Works

Mizjah Ruins is the starting point for instant-respawn farming. The location features dense Mosh'Ogg ogre packs around broken troll-style structures. The key mechanic is that mobs respawn quickly enough to keep you in a constant kill loop instead of waiting for spawn timers.

**Route Pattern:**
1. Fly into the center of Mizjah Ruins
2. Clear the outside packs systematically
3. Move around the stone structures in a loop
4. Circle back before the camp empties out
5. Keep looping the same spawn points

### Why This Farm Works

The value comes from **keeping the kill chain moving** rather than standing in one spot. Each kill roll has a chance to drop transmog or a world-drop item worth listing. The fast respawns ensure you never have real downtime.

### Best Practices

- Use instant AoE abilities to keep pace with respawn rates
- Loot quickly; bring empty bags because old-content farms generate lots of low-value trash
- Check every green item before vendoring—some old-world transmog is valuable
- Post valuable transmog for 24-48 hours rather than panic-undercutting

## Auction House Strategy: How to Turn Loot Into Gold

These instant-respawn farms work because they generate **volume**. But volume alone does not guarantee gold—the business model depends on smart auction-house listing.

### The Long-Tail Transmog Model

- Most individual drops are low value (5-50g)
- Rare transmog pieces can be worth 100g to 10,000g+ depending on realm and item type
- You are betting that out of 1,000 kills, 5-10 will drop something valuable enough to list
- Those 5-10 items, if priced correctly and posted consistently, become your real profit

### Practical Auction House Steps

1. **Use an auction addon** (like TSM, Auctionator, or Dealfinder) to check green items before vendoring
2. **Post valuable transmog for 24-48 hours**, not 12 hours—rare items need time to find the right buyer
3. **Avoid panic-undercutting**: if an item is already listed at 1,000g, do not list yours at 800g just to move it faster
4. **Repost consistently**: valuable transmog may sit for a week before selling, so refresh listings every 1-2 days
5. **Vendor trash after checking**: if an item does not appear in your addon's transmog database, vendor it
6. **Stack materials and sell separately**: Netherweave Cloth stacks sell faster than random greens

### Real Gold vs. Realm Price Variables

The gold per hour you see in the source video is based on:
- The creator's realm's current AH pricing
- High-value rare drops that may not happen every session
- 24-48 hour farmingsessions, not quick 15-minute runs
- Consistent restocking and smart undercutting strategy

**On your realm, results will vary.** Always check your current market prices before committing hours to a farm.

## FAQ: Common Questions About Instant Respawn Farms

**Are these farms guaranteed to make gold?**

No. Instant respawns mean more loot rolls per hour, but actual profit depends on:
- What items actually drop (RNG)
- Current AH prices on your realm (highly variable)
- How quickly you can sell rare items (market timing)

Treat these as inventory-building farms, not guaranteed-gold farms.

**Which farm is best for raw materials?**

**Elemental Plateau** is the clearest material-focused route because the video shows consistent Mote of Fire and Elemental Fragment drops. If you prefer predictable loot, start here.

**Which farms are best for rare transmog?**

**Mizjah Ruins** and **Gordunni Outpost** are positioned as higher-upside transmog farms with visible rare drops. **Path of Conquest** and **Bash'ir Landing** mix transmog with materials.

**What class should I use?**

Use a mobile, high-level character with:
- Instant AoE abilities (to keep pace with respawns)
- Good mobility (Druids, Demon Hunters, Monks, Hunters are strong)
- Sufficient damage to avoid long kill times

Tanks and slow classes will struggle with respawn timing.

**Should I vendor green items or check them first?**

**Check them first.** Some old-world and Outland greens are low value (10-50g), but others are rare transmog worth 500g-5,000g+. Use an auction addon to scan quickly.

**How long should I farm before listing items?**

Farm for at least 30-60 minutes to build a meaningful inventory. Listing 5 items is less efficient than listing 50. The more you farm, the better your odds of hitting high-value drops.

**Can I farm these with a low-level character?**

No. These routes require fast, high-damage AoE to keep pace with respawn rates. Use a level 70+ character (or current expansion max level).

**What if prices are low on my realm?**

Pivot to material-focused farms (Elemental Plateau, Bash'ir Landing) or rotate between farms weekly to avoid market saturation.
